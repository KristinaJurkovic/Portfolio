About me

Creative expression is what I find myself in and what makes me happy in life. Already in elementary school, I realized that I am a visual type of person through detailed observation of colors, shapes, lines, textures, and an interest in typography. So I decided to enroll in high school, specializing in web design, where I had the opportunity to learn more about the combination of colors, space, shapes, typography, and other characteristics of visual communication.

In order to deepen my knowledge in the same field, I enrolled the University North - Professional undergraduate study Multimedia, design and application, and after that, a University graduate studies in Multimedia, after which I gained the title of Master of Science in Multimedia and Graphic Technology.

In addition, I enjoy spending my free time practicing photography, mainly focusing on nature photography and portraits. I also love poetry. :)
